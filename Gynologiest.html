Gynaecologist
Dr. Harsha V. Reddy's professional portfolio encompasses more than 14 years of experience in obstetrics and gynaecology and is seeded in years of intensive training in infertility treatments and advanced laparoscopy, hysteroscopy and sonography techniques. Dr. Reddy is well-respected for her approach to high-risk pregnancies and treatment of infertility cases. She holds an MBBS from Bangalore Medical College and a DGO from KIMS, Bangalore. She also has a DNB in Obstetrics and Gynaecology from St. Martha's Hospital, Bangalore.
